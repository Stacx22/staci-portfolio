Fertility Outcomes Prediction

Built a supervised learning pipeline in R using tidymodels to predict altered fertility outcomes based on lifestyle and health-related features. Compared logistic regression, random forest, and XGBoost models, emphasizing interpretability and ethical handling of health data.

  • Language: R (tidymodels, themis, xgboost)
  • Focus: Classification, model comparison, explainability

About Me

I’m Staci McDuffie, a data science and analytics professional passionate about transforming raw data into actionable insights and compelling visual stories. My work bridges technical rigor and business communication, helping teams make smarter, data-driven decisions.

My Journey

My background blends analytical thinking with a creative approach to problem solving. I’ve built a portfolio of machine learning and visualization projects that address real-world challenges across retail, public safety, agriculture, and transportation. Whether it’s predicting apple quality, forecasting store sales, or uncovering trends in vehicle theft data, I focus on delivering results that combine statistical accuracy with visual clarity.

I’m currently completing advanced coursework in machine learning, big data systems, and data storytelling. My projects have spanned predictive modeling (Linear Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost), time-series forecasting, clustering, and data visualization in both Python and R. Through this work, I’ve developed a strong command of tools such as pandas, scikit-learn, ggplot2, Power BI, and Tableau.

My Philosophy

I believe that the best data solutions don’t just generate numbers—they tell a story. My goal is to make data science accessible by presenting insights through intuitive visuals and clear narratives that resonate with technical and non-technical audiences alike. I’m especially drawn to projects that have measurable impact, such as optimizing operations, improving transparency, and helping organizations understand their customers better.
